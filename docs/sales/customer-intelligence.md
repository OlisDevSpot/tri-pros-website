 The meeting flow needs a Customer Intelligence Layer captured in Phase 1 (Discovery) that drives everything downstream. Right now, none of this is captured digitally. Here's every
  domain detail that matters, organized by when it surfaces:

  Phase 1 — Discovery Intake (Before any pitch)

  Household Context — determines tone, trust needs, and program fit:
  - household_type: senior couple | young family | empty nesters | single owner | adult child present (seniors need trust-first flow; families care about IAQ + comfort)
  - decision_makers_present: both | primary only | partner on phone (if only one → no full close attempt, period)
  - years_in_home: long-term owners have deferred maintenance; new owners may have inherited problems
  - planning_to_sell: changes every outcome statement (from "comfort for years" → "ROI at resale")
  - hoa_present: gates exterior work (roofing, paint, decking) — needs approval timeline

  Home Intelligence — determines which trades are obvious:
  - year_built: pre-1980 = almost certainly needs full energy envelope
  - roof_age_years: >15 yrs = replacement conversation; also gates solar warranty
  - hvac_age_years: >10 yrs = high-SEER replacement
  - window_type: single-pane visible = Energy-Saver qualifier
  - ev_present: massive solar ROI multiplier (locks in energy rate + eliminates fuel cost)
  - pool_spa_present: amplifies solar ROI significantly
  - utility_provider: SCE / LADWP / SoCalGas → determines which rebate programs apply
  - monthly_utility_bill: the single most important number for the Energy-Saver narrative

  Pain & Motivation — the emotional core:
  - primary_pain: roof leak | high utility bill | HVAC failing | aesthetic | foundation | selling soon | storm damage
  - timeline_urgency: immediate | seasonal | eventual → determines how hard to push same-day close
  - trigger_event: what made them call NOW (neighbor's project = social proof already planted)

  Financial Profile — affects close strategy:
  - priority: price | quality | speed (quality-first = never discount; price-first = go to monthly payment immediately)
  - payment_preference: financing signal vs. cash signal
  - budget_anchor: if they stated a number, scope must land inside it
  - prior_contractor_experience: bad experience = trust is the #1 barrier, not price
  - competitor_quotes_obtained: "cheaper quote" objection is coming — prep apples-to-apples
  - tax_filer_type: seniors on SS may owe no federal taxes → IRA credit may not apply to them (critical to not over-promise)
