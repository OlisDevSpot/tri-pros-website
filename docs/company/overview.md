# Company Overview — Tri Pros Remodeling

## Identity

- **Legal name**: Tri Pros Remodeling
- **Location**: Southern California
- **Industry**: Residential construction and remodeling
- **Market**: Homeowners in Southern California metro areas

## Mission & Brand Promise

Tri Pros Remodeling exists to give homeowners access to skilled, trustworthy, licensed contractors — and to deliver remodeling projects that increase the comfort, value, and efficiency of their homes. We do not cut corners. Our reputation is built on showing up, doing what we said, and standing behind our work after completion.

## Team Composition

TPR operates as a lean, high-performance team combining three core competencies:

- **Technology** — Systems, tools, and digital infrastructure that make our sales process faster and more professional than most contractors
- **Business** — Sales process, CRM management, customer communication, and operational excellence
- **Construction** — Field execution, trade expertise, subcontractor relationships, quality control

This is our advantage over single-trade competitors: we combine the polish of a tech-forward company with the depth of experienced construction professionals.

## What We Do

TPR focuses on two primary project categories:

### Energy-Efficient Remodeling
Projects that reduce energy consumption, qualify for tax credits/rebates, and generate ongoing monthly savings for the homeowner:
- Roofing (cool roof / energy-efficient shingles)
- Insulation
- Windows & Doors
- HVAC
- Solar

### General Remodeling
Projects that improve livability, aesthetics, or structural integrity:
- Bathroom remodels
- Kitchen remodels
- Flooring
- Paint
- Decking
- Foundation

## How We Generate Leads

TPR's lead generation is outbound-first:

1. **Telemarketing** — Targeted calls to homeowners in our service area
2. **Social media** — Paid and organic campaigns driving inbound inquiries

Both channels funnel into **in-home appointments** — the primary sales event where an agent visits the customer's home to assess, present, and close.

## Revenue Model at a Glance

```
Lead Gen (telemarketing + social)
  → Appointment Set
    → In-Home Meeting (agent visits)
      → Proposal Created & Sent
        → Signed Contract (via DocuSign)
          → Project Delivered
```
