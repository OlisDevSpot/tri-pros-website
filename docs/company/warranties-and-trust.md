## Permit & Inspection Process

TPR pulls all required permits for permitted work. This matters to homeowners for two reasons:

1. **Resale**: Unpermitted work must be disclosed and can fail a buyer's inspection
2. **Insurance**: Homeowners insurance may deny claims related to unpermitted work

When TPR pulls a permit, a city inspector validates the work — providing an independent confirmation that the installation was done correctly.
