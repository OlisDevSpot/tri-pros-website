# Services Catalog — Tri Pros Remodeling

## Trade Categories

### 1. Roofing
**What it includes**: Full roof replacement or repair. Shingle, tile, or flat roof systems. Underlayment, decking inspection/repair, flashing, ventilation.

**Materials offered**: Architectural shingles, cool-roof reflective shingles, tile, TPO/flat systems.

**Typical outcomes**:
- Extended roof lifespan (20–50 years depending on material)
- Improved energy efficiency (cool roof systems reflect heat)
- Leak prevention and structural protection
- Enhanced curb appeal and home value

**Classification**: Energy-efficient (cool roof) / General remodeling (standard replacement)

---

### 2. HVAC
**What it includes**: Heating and air conditioning system installation or replacement. Ductwork assessment and sealing. Smart thermostat integration.

**Materials offered**: High-efficiency split systems, heat pumps, ducted and ductless mini-splits.

**Typical outcomes**:
- Lower monthly utility bills (high-SEER systems)
- More consistent home comfort
- Reduced carbon footprint
- Qualifies for rebates and tax credits (energy-efficient units)

**Classification**: Energy-efficient

---

### 3. Windows & Doors
**What it includes**: Single or full-home window replacement. Exterior door replacement. Sliding glass doors. Installation includes trim, sealing, and finishing.

**Materials offered**: Dual-pane, triple-pane, Low-E glass; vinyl, fiberglass, or aluminum frames.

**Typical outcomes**:
- Reduced heat gain/loss (lower utility bills)
- Noise reduction
- Improved home security
- Enhanced aesthetics and natural light

**Classification**: Energy-efficient

---

### 4. Insulation
**What it includes**: Attic, wall, and crawlspace insulation installation or upgrade. Air sealing.

**Materials offered**: Blown-in fiberglass, blown-in cellulose, spray foam, batt insulation.

**Typical outcomes**:
- Significant reduction in heating/cooling costs
- More consistent indoor temperatures
- Reduction in drafts and cold spots
- Qualifies for utility rebates and federal tax credits

**Classification**: Energy-efficient

---

### 5. Solar
**What it includes**: Rooftop solar panel system installation. Inverter, monitoring system, and utility interconnection.

**Materials offered**: Monocrystalline panels, microinverters, string inverters, battery storage options.

**Typical outcomes**:
- Monthly electricity bill reduction or elimination
- Fixed energy costs for 25+ years
- Federal Investment Tax Credit (ITC) eligibility
- Increased home resale value

**Classification**: Energy-efficient

---

### 6. Foundation
**What it includes**: Foundation repair, leveling, crack injection, waterproofing, pier systems.

**Materials offered**: Helical piers, push piers, carbon fiber straps, polyurethane injection.

**Typical outcomes**:
- Structural stability and safety
- Prevention of further settling or cracking
- Protection of home value
- Peace of mind for homeowner and future buyers

**Classification**: General remodeling

---

### 7. Bathroom Remodel
**What it includes**: Full or partial bathroom renovation. Shower/tub replacement, vanity, tile, flooring, lighting, plumbing fixture upgrades.

**Materials offered**: Acrylic surrounds, tile, stone, luxury vinyl plank (LVP), frameless glass enclosures.

**Typical outcomes**:
- Increased daily comfort and functionality
- Significant increase in home resale value
- Modernized aesthetic

**Classification**: General remodeling

---

### 8. Kitchen Remodel
**What it includes**: Cabinet replacement or refacing, countertops, backsplash, appliance coordination, flooring, lighting.

**Materials offered**: Quartz/granite countertops, soft-close cabinetry, tile backsplash.

**Typical outcomes**:
- Dramatically improved functionality
- Highest ROI of any home improvement (kitchen remodels return 60–80% at resale)
- Daily lifestyle upgrade

**Classification**: General remodeling

---

### 9. Flooring
**What it includes**: Full floor replacement for one or more rooms. Subfloor prep included.

**Materials offered**: Luxury vinyl plank (LVP), hardwood, engineered hardwood, tile, laminate.

**Typical outcomes**:
- Updated aesthetics throughout the home
- Improved durability and ease of maintenance
- Increased home value

**Classification**: General remodeling

---

### 10. Paint (Interior / Exterior)
**What it includes**: Interior room painting or full exterior painting. Includes prep, priming, and finish coats.

**Materials offered**: Premium zero-VOC and standard latex paints. Various finish levels (matte, eggshell, satin).

**Typical outcomes**:
- Refreshed, modernized appearance
- Protection of surfaces from wear and weather (exterior)
- High perceived value for relatively low investment

**Classification**: General remodeling

---

### 11. Decking
**What it includes**: New deck construction or existing deck replacement/restoration.

**Materials offered**: Composite decking (Trex-style), pressure-treated wood, hardwood.

**Typical outcomes**:
- Expanded outdoor living space
- Increased home value and curb appeal

**Classification**: General remodeling

## Project Type Classification

**Energy-Efficient Projects** (utility savings, rebates, tax credit angle):
Roofing (cool roof), HVAC, Windows & Doors, Insulation, Solar

**General Remodeling Projects** (comfort, aesthetics, home value):
Foundation, Bathroom, Kitchen, Flooring, Paint, Decking

> Note: Many customers combine energy-efficient and general remodeling scopes in a single project. Presenting complementary scopes together (e.g., insulation + HVAC, or roofing + solar) increases contract size and delivers compounding ROI to the customer.
