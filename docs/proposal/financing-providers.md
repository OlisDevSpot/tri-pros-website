# Financing Providers — Master Research & Application Tracker

> **Purpose**: Compiled list of consumer home-improvement financing providers Tri Pros can enroll in as a contractor/dealer partner, so we can offer financing to homeowners at the point of sale. Built to maximize the number of approval paths for our customers (prime + near-prime + sub-prime) across **unsecured installment loans**, **revolving credit**, and **secured/HELOC** products.
>
> **Status**: Research compiled 2026-06-07. Criteria marked _"not published"_ must be confirmed at application — most lenders gate their exact dealer minimums behind the application/rep call. Treat published numbers as the source of truth and **verify at enrollment**.
>
> **Currently active for Tri Pros**: 360 Finance (by Modernize), Figure (HELOC).

---

## How to read this

Providers are tiered by **how hard they are to qualify for** — apply to Tier 1 first (instant / no minimums), bank those approvals, then work up to the tiers with sales-volume gates as our documented volume grows.

| Financing type | What it means for the customer |
|---|---|
| **Unsecured installment** | Fixed monthly payment, no lien on the home. The bread-and-butter for remodeling. Same-as-cash and reduced-APR promos common. |
| **Revolving credit** | A branded credit-card line (e.g. Synchrony HOME). Promo financing 6–60 mo. Good for smaller tickets + repeat work. |
| **Secured / HELOC** | Lien against home equity. Lowest rates, larger amounts, slower. (Figure, PACE.) |
| **Marketplace / broker** | One application shops many lenders → highest approval odds, widest credit spectrum. Usually free or low-fee to us. |

**Dealer fee** = the discount fee the lender takes per funded job (roughly 0%–15% depending on the promo). Lower fee ≈ better promos for customer cost us more. This is the real "cost" of offering financing — not sign-up fees.

No meaningful sales-volume gate. Mostly free to join. Fast onboarding. These give us the widest credit coverage immediately.

| **Wisetack** | Marketplace (unsecured) | No setup fee, no minimum volume. 3.9% per financed txn. ~5 biz-day approval; bank link via Plaid. | $500–$25K | wisetack.com/merchants/home-services. support@wisetack.com / (833) 927-0333 |

| **PowerPay** | Lender + marketplace (unsecured) | Home improvement + healthcare. No published volume minimum. | Fast approvals, monthly pay | getpowerpay.com |

**Note on marketplaces vs. direct lenders**: Acorn, Wisetack, Enhancify, SuperMoney, Finturf, FinMkt, Hearth are **aggregators** — one enrollment unlocks many lenders. Enrolling in 2–3 of these covers a huge chunk of the credit spectrum with minimal effort. Direct lenders (Aqua, Service Finance, GoodLeap, Synchrony, Regions) give better promo terms and are what the customer sees as "the bank."

## Application order (recommended)

1. **Week 1 — Tier 1 marketplaces (free, instant):** Acorn → Wisetack → Aqua → Enhancify → SuperMoney → Finturf. Each is a short online form; together they cover the full credit spectrum within days.
2. **Week 1–2 — Tier 2 direct lenders (better promos):** Service Finance, Synchrony HOME, GoodLeap, Dividend, Foundation Finance (Foundation = our subprime safety net).
3. **Week 2 — Bank-backed unsecured:** Medallion Bank, Slice by FNBO.
4. **When volume documented:** Regions HIF (need 5 yr + $500K), then ChargeAfter / any $2M-gate programs.
5. **Niche, as needed:** Lyon Financial (if pool/outdoor), keep Figure for equity deals.

**Diversification rule**: enroll in at least one marketplace + one strong prime direct lender (Service Finance/GoodLeap) + one subprime specialist (Foundation Finance). That trio alone approves most customers.
