## What's locked (all 11 corrections)

The full lock detail lives in [`EPIC.md` decisions log → 2026-05-31 to 2026-06-01](./EPIC.md#decisions-log). Bullet summary:

1. **CT API paths corrected** — most of `providers/cloudtalk/lib/*.ts` was fictional. Real paths: `PUT /contacts/add.json`, `POST /contacts/edit/{contactId}.json`, `PUT /contacts/addTags/{contactId}.json`, `DELETE /contacts/removeTags/{contactId}.json`, `POST /campaigns/edit/{id}.json` (status: active/inactive), `GET /calls/{callId}` (no `.json`), `POST /sms/send.json`, `POST /bulk/contacts.json`. Verb pattern: `PUT add` / `POST edit` / `DELETE delete` / `GET index|show`.
2. **Tag-as-membership** — no enroll/unenroll endpoint. Enrollment = `addTags Campaign-MetaAds`. Per CT docs, **tags persist past exhaustion**. Two tag categories: membership (`Campaign-MetaAds`, `Campaign-HomeDepot`) + lifecycle (1:1 with `voipCampaignStatus` enum).
3. **Tag-write discipline** — on `Exhausted`: KEEP `Campaign-X` (historical). On `Booked`/`DoNotCall`: **REMOVE `Campaign-X`** (defense-in-depth against attempts-counter reset). Historical "was-in-source-X" visibility lives in our DB (`customers.voipCampaignSource` column, Phase 2).
4. **Webhook architecture: 5 Workflow Automations, not centralized endpoint.** Object+Action pairs: `Call.Started`, `Call.Answered`, `Call.Ended`, `Call.Modified` (disposition arrives here, NOT on ended), `Messages.Received`. All POST to `/api/webhooks/cloudtalk?secret=...`. Body constructed by us in CT's body builder using `{{ event.properties.X }}` syntax; `event_type` injected as first body field (CT doesn't add it).
5. **DID strategy = Pattern A shared pool.** All 6 DIDs assigned to BOTH Oliver + Sean. Both campaigns: Smart Rotation. Per-campaign DID brand separation NOT achievable (Q10 corrected). Tradeoff accepted.
6. **Cadence Q5 finalized:** 10 attempts × 3hr intervals (~30hr dialing time, 3–4 calendar days).
7. **Agent IDs DROPPED (Q3 inversion).** CT agents independent from app users. Remove `CT_FAVORITE_AGENT_ID_*` env vars + `assignFavoriteAgent()` lib function.
8. **Final attribute set: 3 custom + CT built-ins.**
   - Custom: `Lead Source`, `Primary Trade`, `Trades Interested`
   - Built-in: CT's `Contact.name` (full), `Contact.city`
   - DROPPED: `program_name`, `program_offer`, `hd_endorser`, `hd_project_label`, custom `first_name`/`last_name` (using CT built-in)
   - DEFERRED Phase 2: HD analytics attrs (`home_depot_request_id` etc.)
9. **Two new DB tables:** `voip_campaigns` (CT identity bridge: source_slug, ct_campaign_id, ct_membership_tag, ct_status, attempts_per_contact, hours_between_attempts) + `voip_contact_attributes` (app_key, ct_attribute_id, ct_title). Sync via admin-triggered "Resync from CloudTalk" button (Phase 1); daily cron deferred to Phase 2 if drift observed.
10. **`lead_sources.voipConfigJSON.campaigns.{enabled, autoEnroll}` retained** for APP-side policy (separate concern from CT-identity in `voip_campaigns`).
11. **openapi-ts hand-typed (LOCKED, do not retry).** `@hey-api/openapi-ts@0.97.3` codegen-core errors on CT's swagger. Probes 1–4 done. Hand-typing wins for the small Phase 1 surface. `@hey-api/openapi-ts` dev dep removed.
